Melty Chicken Quesadilla

An authentic mexican delight! Cook and serve in just a few minutes. Serve with salsa.

Perfect for the whole family!

Ingredients

  • Flour Tortillas
  • 4-Cheese Blend
  • Chicken Breast

Directions

  1. Heat Pan
  2. Add Cheese and Chicken to Flour Tortillas
  3. Add to Pan and Cover
  4. Flip after 3 Minutes
